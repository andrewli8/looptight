# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is a welcoming, harassment-free space for everyone, regardless of
experience, identity, or background. We expect participants to be respectful,
assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on the work.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include harassment, personal attacks, demeaning
comments, and publishing others' private information without consent.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions) and when an
individual represents the project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the maintainer through GitHub. Reports are
reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions,
or restrict participation, for behavior they judge inappropriate.

This document adapts the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org).
